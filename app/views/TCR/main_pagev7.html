Tax Credits Change of Circumstances

Use the following sections to tell us about changes to your circumstances. You will need to confirm your changes before sending.

  • Personal and partner details Change: Your name, address and relationship status

  • Work and hours Change: Where you work and the number of hours

  • Income Change: How much you earn from work and benefits

  • Children Change: The children who are on your claim

  • Childcare Change: Who looks after your children and the costs

  • Bank and payment details Change: the bank account where you receive your payments

  • Report a death Tell us if someone in your household has died
