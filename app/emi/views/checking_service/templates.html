Employment Related Securities

From:
HM Revenue and Customs
History:
Updated 11 August 2014
Part of:
Employment Related Securities

HM Revenue and Customs (HMRC) have provided templates for you to record the supporting information for your Company Share Option Plan (CSOP) annual return. The templates are available in Open Document Spreadsheet (ODS), Comma Separated Values (CSV) and Microsoft Excel spreadsheet (XLS) file formats.

Find out more about open standards for government.

Download CSV template 20KB

This download contains 5 CSV templates in a zip file.

Download XLS template 3KB

This download is a Microsoft Excel spreadsheet.

Detail

Don't:

  • change the order of the columns
  • delete any columns
  • add extra columns
  • change any sheet names

Only ODS, CSV or XLS files can be uploaded as part of your CSOP annual return.
