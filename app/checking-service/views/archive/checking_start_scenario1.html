Employment Related Securities (ERS).

Check your CSOP spreadsheets

Use this service to check for formatting errors in your Company Share Option Plan (CSOP) spreadsheets.

Checking a CSOP spreadsheet after you update it allows you to identify and then correct formatting errors. This makes it easier to submit your return at the end of the tax year.

Before you start

You'll need to know where the spreadsheet that you want to check is stored.

Your spreadsheet needs to be in an OpenDocument Spreadsheet format (ODS file).

ODS files are a type of spreadsheet that makes sharing or collaborating with government documents easier.

The time it takes to check a file depends on the speed of your internet connection and the size of your spreadsheet.
